  <h1>CardVault</h1>

  <p>
    CardVault is a secure credit card management application designed to help users track their credit card bills, due dates, and payment reminders.
  </p>

  <h2>What CardVault Does</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Automatically reads billing emails from your Gmail account (with your permission)</li>
    <li>Extracts credit card bill details such as due date and amount</li>
    <li>Sends reminders and notifications to avoid late payments</li>
    <li>Allows management of multiple credit cards in one place</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>How We Use Your Data</h2>
  <p>
    CardVault accesses Gmail data only to identify and extract credit card billing information. 
    We do not store, share, or use your emails for any purpose other than providing bill tracking and reminders.
  </p>
